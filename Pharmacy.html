An online pharmacy is a digital platform where customers can purchase medications and health-related products over the internet. It provides convenience by allowing users to browse, order, and receive prescriptions and over-the-counter drugs without visiting a physical store. These platforms often offer services like online consultations with licensed pharmacists or healthcare providers, ensuring prescriptions are valid and appropriate. Online pharmacies can provide competitive pricing, discreet delivery, and access to a broader range of products. However, users must choose reputable providers to ensure product authenticity and compliance with legal standards. They play a vital role in modern, accessible healthcare solutions.
